\textbf{Problem.}
Prove or refute:

\[
\forall \Delta \ \exists R,C>0:\ 
\operatorname{tr}((\Delta^{(1)}_{X_R(G)})^\dagger)\le C
\]
for all finite vertex-transitive $G$ with $\deg(G)\le\Delta$.

Equivalently:
\[
\lambda_1(\mathrm{Lk}_R(v))\ge \varepsilon>0 \quad \forall v.
\]
